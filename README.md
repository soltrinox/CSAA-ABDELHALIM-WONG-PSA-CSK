## The Four Cases

### Overview

On April 23, 2025, a jury returned a 9-3 defense verdict -- the minimum civil majority. Plaintiff alleges the verdict was procured through extrinsic fraud: defense counsel made false representations to the court about receipt and possession of critical evidence, causing the court to deny continuances and exclude material evidence based on those false representations. When defense counsel casually admitted on Trial Day 7 that the body-worn camera footage was "provided by Dolan," the artifice collapsed. The transcript captured both the lie and the confession.

Plaintiff constructed three independent post-trial proceedings, each with its own legal theory, decision-maker, standard of review, and independent remedy lane -- engineered so that a successful defense posture in one case automatically generates a concession or adverse inference in the others.

| # | Proceeding | Case No. | Forum | Theory |
|---|------------|----------|-------|--------|
| 1 | **[Appeal](#1-appeal--a173827)** | A173827 | Court of Appeal, 1st Dist., Div. 3 | Instructional error, evidentiary error, juror misconduct |
| 2 | **[Equity Action](#2-equity-action--cgc-25-631801)** | CGC-25-631801 | SF Superior Court | Independent action to set aside judgment for extrinsic fraud |
| 3 | **[Insurance Fraud / UCL](#3-insurance-fraud--ucl--cgc-25-631802)** | CGC-25-631802 | SF Superior Court | CSAA's pre-litigation false denial letter; bad faith; UCL |
| 4 | **[Underlying Trial](#4-underlying-trial--cgc-21-594102)** | CGC-21-594102 | SF Superior Court | Pedestrian-vehicle collision; 9-3 defense verdict |

### 4. Underlying Trial -- CGC-21-594102

**Forum:** San Francisco Superior Court, Dept. 504 (Hon. Garrett L. Wong)
**Verdict:** 9-3 defense verdict, April 23, 2025

Plaintiff Franciscus Dylan Rosario was struck by a vehicle driven by Defendant Subhi Abdelhalim in a San Francisco crosswalk on February 4, 2021. The driver called 911 and admitted striking the pedestrian. The jury returned a 9-3 defense verdict -- the minimum civil majority -- after the defense made false representations to the court that caused the exclusion and delay of critical evidence.

**Operational linchpin:** On April 16, 2025, defense counsel stated: *"Sure. They were provided by Dolan. He has had them for whatever, sure."* This admission is irreconcilable with her prior sworn representations that the materials were "not produced during discovery" and of "unknown provenance."

## The Novel: Rosario v. Goliath

*"I barely hit him." Four words on a 911 call. The driver's confession. Twenty-one days later, an insurer denied without ever hearing it. Four years later, defense counsel told the court it was "news to me." The transcript captured both the lie and the confession.*

This is the story of how one brain-injured plaintiff, proceeding alone against a multi-billion-dollar insurer and its retained law firms, built from the defense's own record an architecture of accountability that turns every defense posture into a concession.
