### Court Reliance on False Representations

**[COURT-RELIANCE-FRAUD-ON-COURT.md](COURT-RELIANCE-FRAUD-ON-COURT.md)**

Full analysis of how the trial court relied on defense counsel's false representations to make procedural and evidentiary rulings that shaped the entire trial. Documents the five specific instances of institutional reliance, the causal chain from misrepresentation to ruling to prejudice, and the equity framework for vacatur. Includes the damages architecture and the continuing-harm analysis.

**Key topics:** Extrinsic fraud elements -- Three-Leg Framework -- Court reliance matrix -- Continuance denial -- MIL grants -- Supplemental report exclusion -- 911 deferral -- Institutional reliance anatomy -- Damages categories -- Sanctions (CCP sec. 128.7)

**Related case:** CGC-25-631801 (Equity Action)
